Raw Cotton Bales

At Jaya Cotton, our raw cotton bales are a product of meticulous processing to ensure top-notch quality and reliability. The journey begins with procuring raw cotton, which is then thoroughly cleaned and processed using our advanced ginning machines. These machines efficiently separate the lint from the seeds, resulting in lint that is finely pressed into bales weighing 170 kgs each.

We understand the importance of maintaining product integrity during transportation, which is why our bales are tightly packed with high-quality materials. This careful packaging not only protects the cotton but also ensures smooth transportation to our clients.

Cotton Seed

At Jaya Cotton, we are committed to delivering high-quality cotton seeds specifically suited for oil extraction. Cotton seeds are a valuable by-product of the cotton ginning process, rich in oil and nutrients, making them an essential raw material for oil mills.

The Process

Once cotton lint is separated from the seeds during the ginning process, the seeds are cleaned and prepared for oil extraction. Cottonseed oil, known for its versatility, is a premium product in the food industry, used in snacks, salad dressings, and cooking applications. The process involves flaking the seeds and then extracting the oil, ensuring maximum yield and purity.

Our Varieties

We offer cotton seeds that are ideal for oil mills, ensuring they meet the highest industry standards. Our seeds are rich in oil content and are meticulously processed to ensure they are free from contaminants.

Cotton Seed Cake (UD)

At Jaya Cotton, we offer premium-quality cotton seed cake, a by-product of cottonseed oil extraction, known for its high nutritional value and versatile applications. Our cotton seed cake is an excellent source of protein and energy, making it a valuable addition to livestock feed.

    Benefits of Cotton Seed Cake

  • High Protein Content: Our cotton seed cake contains a high percentage of protein, essential for the growth and health of livestock.
  • Rich in Nutrients: It is packed with essential nutrients, including fiber and minerals, promoting better digestion and overall health of animals.
  • Energy-Rich Feed: Cotton seed cake is an excellent source of energy, helping to maintain the vigor and productivity of livestock.
  • Sustainable and Cost-Effective: Utilizing cotton seed cake as feed is a sustainable practice that reduces waste and offers a cost-effective alternative to other feed options.
