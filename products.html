Raw Cotton Bales

The raw cotton procured is first cleaned and then processed by our highly modernized ginning machines, which separates the lint from the seed and the lint is finely pressed into bales of 170 kgs each. Packing a product with right material helps in maintaining the quality of the product as it is produced. Our bales are tightly packed with good quality material which helps in smooth transportation. Cotton Seed

Cotton seed makes up about 65% of the raw cotton weight. After separating the cotton seed from lint, we process some seed in our oil mills and the seed that is not processed further is sold to de-lint& solvent mills and oil expellers

Cotton Seed Cake (UD)

After extracting the oil, the left-over residue called cotton seed cake is soldfor cattle feed.
