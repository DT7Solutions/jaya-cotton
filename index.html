About Company

Established - 2002

Jaya Cotton

Jaya Cotton, established in 2002 by Mr.N.Subbarao, is the leading Cotton Ginning Manufacturing Industry in the last 17 years. The company is specialized in processing Raw Cotton and Supply Cotton Lint to the leading spinning mills and textile industries across India and other countries. With two T.M.C. units (18 gin and 24 gin), having a capacity to process 1000 bales a day, Jaya Cotton has been one of the pioneers in Guntur’s Cotton market in the last 17 years. The company’s integrity and credibility in providing quality and timely services built long-lasting client relationships with India’s renowned industries, some of which are Arvind, Vardhaman, Welspun, Bombay Dying, and many more.
